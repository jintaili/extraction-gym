About this coffee
This lot was delivered by growers farming small garden plots around the Danche kebele. Fresh cherry is sorted on arrival, depulped, fermented under clean water, washed through channels, and dried slowly on raised beds. We taste florals up front, a ripe white peach sweetness, Meyer lemon brightness, and a honeyed black tea finish.
