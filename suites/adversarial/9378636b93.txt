Tasting notes: Strawberry jam, jasmine, cacao nib
Roast level: Light

Producer: Smallholder farmers delivering to Bombe Washing Station
Country: Ethiopia
Region: Bensa, Sidama
Process: Natural
Variety: 74158, Kurume
Altitude: 1,920–2,050 masl
Harvest: 2024/2025

This lot comes from smallholder gardens around Bombe in the Bensa district of Sidama. Ripe cherries are delivered daily to Bombe Washing Station, where the team sorts by hand before a slow natural drying on raised beds. We taste a syrupy cup with big red-fruit sweetness, a floral lift, and a clean cocoa finish.

We roast Ethiopia Bombe Natural lightly for filter brewing. It shines as a pour over or batch brew after a short rest of 7–14 days from roast date.
