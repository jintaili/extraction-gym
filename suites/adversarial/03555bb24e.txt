This small lot from Ana Maria Castro is the kind of clean, floral Colombian coffee we look forward to every year. Pink Bourbon grown on the upper slopes of Finca La Primavera is hand picked, floated for density, fermented for 28 hours in sealed tanks, and washed before a slow parabolic drying stage on raised beds.

In the cup we find white peach and jasmine up front, with panela sweetness and a delicate citrus finish. We roast this coffee for filter brewing, with enough development for clarity and sweetness without muting the florals.
