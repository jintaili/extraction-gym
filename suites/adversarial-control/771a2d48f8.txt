This lot comes from the hills above the town of Santa Barbara in Antioquia, Colombia, where Maria Isabel Henao cultivates Pink Bourbon on the small family farm Finca El Cedro. The cooler nights at this elevation help stretch maturation, giving the cup a bright, layered sweetness.

After hand-sorting ripe cherries, the coffee was pulped, fermented in tiled tanks for 28 hours, washed with clean spring water, and dried slowly on raised beds. We roasted this lot for filter brewing to highlight its crisp red-apple acidity, florals, and a panela-like finish.

Brew suggestion: 16g coffee / 260g water, 94°C, medium-fine grind.
