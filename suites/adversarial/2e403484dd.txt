This AA lot from Gichathaini is exactly why we keep coming back to Nyeri: a bright blackcurrant sweetness, pink-grapefruit acidity, and a clean turbinado finish. We roast it for clarity and sweetness on filter brewers, with enough solubility for a lively iced pour-over.

Gichathaini Factory is one of the washing stations operated by the Gikanda Farmers Cooperative Society near Karatina in Mathira. Smallholder members deliver ripe cherry, which is depulped, fermented, washed in fresh water, and dried slowly on raised beds. This coffee is not flavored, infused, or co-fermented.

Brew starting point: 16g coffee / 250g water, 93°C, medium-fine grind. Expect the cup to open up after 7 days off roast.
