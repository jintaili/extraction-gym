Process: Natural
Variety: 74110, 74112
Altitude: 1,950–2,200 masl
Harvest: November 2024–January 2025
Roast level: Light

This lot comes from smallholder deliveries to Chelbesa Washing Station in Gedeb. Ripe cherries were floated, sorted by hand, and dried slowly on raised beds with careful turning through the hottest parts of the day.

In the cup: a high-toned natural with strawberry jam up front, bergamot aromatics, and a clean cocoa-nib finish. We roast this coffee for clarity and sweetness, best enjoyed as filter or a gentle immersion brew.

Brew guide
V60: 18 g coffee / 300 g water, 94°C, total brew time 2:45–3:00.
Rest recommendation: 7–14 days off roast.
