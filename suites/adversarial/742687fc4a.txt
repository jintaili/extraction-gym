This lot comes to us through Benti Nenka washing station in Hambela Wamena, where smallholder growers cultivate coffee on steep garden plots shaded by false banana and native trees. Ripe cherries are hand-sorted and dried slowly on raised beds, with careful turning during the warm afternoons and covered resting overnight.

In the cup we find a vivid natural profile: blueberry up front, a jasmine-like floral lift, and a clean lemon candy finish. We roast this coffee lightly to keep the fruit bright and the texture silky rather than jammy.

Brew notes
For a 250g bag, we like 16g coffee to 260g water, 94°C, with a total brew time around 2:45–3:10.
