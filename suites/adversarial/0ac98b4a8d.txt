Tasting notes
Blackcurrant, blood orange, brown sugar

Roast: Filter

Country: Kenya
Region: Nyeri County, Tetu
Process: Washed
Variety: SL28, SL34, Ruiru 11, Batian
Altitude: 1,700 - 1,900 masl
Harvest: December 2024 - January 2025

This lot comes from Gachatha Factory in Tetu, Nyeri, one of the classic growing areas on the slopes of the Aberdare range. Smallholder members deliver ripe cherry to the station, where it is carefully sorted before pulping, dry fermentation, washing in clean channels and slow drying on raised beds.

In the cup we find everything we love about a fresh Kenya AA: vivid blackcurrant acidity, a blood-orange brightness and a soft brown-sugar finish. We roast this coffee for filter brewing to keep the fruit clear and the sweetness balanced.

Brewing suggestion
V60: 15g coffee / 250g water / 94C / 2:45-3:15 total brew time.
