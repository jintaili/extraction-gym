At first glance this lot reads like a classic Santa Ana single origin: clean structure, highland sweetness, and the familiar cocoa finish we love from the Apaneca-Ilamatepec range. We built the profile to highlight that regional character in a balanced filter roast.

Lot composition: 60% washed Bourbon from Finca Las Brumas and 40% natural Pacamara from Finca El Manzano. Both farms are in the Santa Ana department and were cupped separately before being combined post-roast to give the cup a more layered fruit profile without losing the honeyed clarity of the washed component.

In the cup we find red apple and honeycomb up front, with cocoa nib and a soft dried-fruit accent as it cools. This is sweet and approachable on pour-over, but has enough density for an elegant AeroPress.
