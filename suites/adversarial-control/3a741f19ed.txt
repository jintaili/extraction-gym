This lot is a sparkling Pink Bourbon from Finca La Esperanza in Palestina, Huila. María Fernanda Ospina and her family have been refining their picking and drying protocols for small separated lots, and this white honey process keeps the cup intensely sweet while showing the florals we love in this variety.

Ripe cherries are hand sorted, depulped with a thin layer of mucilage left intact, then dried slowly on raised beds under shade for 18-22 days. In the cup we find apricot jam, orange blossom and a soft panela sweetness, with a silky body and a clean citrus finish.

Recommended for: V60, Kalita, AeroPress and batch brew. We roast this coffee for filter brewing rather than espresso.
