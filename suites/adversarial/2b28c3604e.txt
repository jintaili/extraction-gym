This lot comes from smallholder farmers delivering ripe cherry to Konga Wete washing station near Yirgacheffe. After sorting, the coffee is depulped, fermented in water, washed clean, and dried slowly on raised beds. We roast it for clarity and sweetness, keeping the cup bright but not sharp.

In the cup we find a delicate floral opening, lemon curd acidity, white peach, and a honeyed black tea finish. Best brewed as pour-over, AeroPress, or batch brew.
