A bright and layered lot from Gachatha Factory in Nyeri, selected for its sparkling acidity and clean sweetness. Expect a classic Kenyan cup profile: blackcurrant up front, pink grapefruit through the middle, and a long cane-sugar finish as it cools.

Gachatha Factory is part of the Gachatha Farmers Cooperative Society and receives cherry from smallholder members farming on the fertile slopes between the Aberdare range and Mount Kenya. After careful sorting, the coffee is fully washed, fermented, and dried slowly on raised beds to preserve clarity.

We roast this coffee for filter brewing, with enough development to bring sweetness without muting the fruit. Recommended for V60, Kalita, AeroPress, and batch brew.
