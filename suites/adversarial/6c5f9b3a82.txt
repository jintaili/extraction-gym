This lot from El Diviso is all about saturated fruit and a sparkling finish. We taste ripe strawberry jam up front, a bright pink lemonade acidity through the middle, and a soft cacao nib note as it cools. It is roasted for filter brewing, though adventurous espresso drinkers may enjoy it with a longer ratio.

Nestor Lasso and the team at El Diviso have become known for carefully separated microlots grown around Bruselas in Huila. For this Sidra selection, ripe cherries were floated, hand sorted, and dried as a natural process lot on raised beds with close attention to temperature and airflow. No co-ferments, added fruit, or flavorings are used in this coffee.

Brew recipe
We like 15g coffee to 250g water at 93°C, medium-fine grind, total brew time 2:45–3:15.
