Raro Boda is the kind of washed Ethiopia that made us fall in love with coffee in the first place: fragrant, clean, and lifted. Lots from family gardens around Uraga are delivered to the Raro Boda station, where ripe cherry is sorted, depulped, fermented under water, and dried slowly on raised beds.

In the cup we find jasmine aromatics, ripe apricot sweetness, a flash of bergamot, and a honeyed finish. We roast this coffee light for clarity and sparkle; it is intended for pour-over, batch brew, and other filter methods.

Brew starting point: 16g coffee, 260g water, 3:00-3:20 total brew time.
