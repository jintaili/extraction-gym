A tiny bag for deciding, a full bag for falling in love. The selector above opens on our 50g sampler; choose 250g or 1kg if you already know this coffee is your thing.

Tasting notes: Rainier cherry, orange blossom, panela
Roast: Filter roast
Origin: Colombia
Region: Huila, Palestina
Producer: Finca El Mirador — Ana Lucia Trujillo
Process: washed
Variety: Pink Bourbon
Altitude: 1,850–1,950 masl
Harvest: June–August 2025

This fresh lot from Ana Lucia Trujillo at Finca El Mirador is the clean, florality-first side of Pink Bourbon that we chase every year. Expect ripe red cherry and a lifted orange-blossom aroma, rounded out by panela sweetness as the cup cools.

Ana Lucia's family farm sits above Palestina in southern Huila, where cool nights help stretch maturation. Cherries were hand sorted, depulped the same day, fermented in tiled tanks for 28 hours, washed with spring water, and dried slowly on raised beds under shade cloth.

Best for: V60, Kalita, Origami, April Brewer, and other filter methods. We do not roast this as an espresso profile, though adventurous dialers may enjoy it as a bright single-origin shot.
