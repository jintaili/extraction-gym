This fresh lot from Finca La Bolsa is the kind of clean, structured Guatemala we love to keep on the menu through late spring. Jorge Vides' team hand-sorted ripe cherry from the Bourbon and Caturra blocks above La Libertad, then depulped, fermented in clean water, washed and dried the parchment slowly on raised beds.

In the cup we find crisp red apple acidity, soft orange blossom aromatics and a long caramel sweetness. It is roasted on our filter profile for clarity, but it has enough body to make a sweet, balanced immersion brew as well.

Brew suggestion: 16g coffee to 260g water, 94°C, medium grind. Rest 7–14 days after roast date.
