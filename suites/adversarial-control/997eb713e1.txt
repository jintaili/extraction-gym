This season's Bombe Natural is all about ripe red fruit and florals without tipping into boozy territory. We roast it light for filter brewing to keep the cup lifted: strawberry jam up front, a jasmine-like aroma as it cools, and a rounded milk chocolate finish.

This lot was assembled from Bombe smallholder farmers delivering ripe cherries to Bombe Washing Station in Bensa, Sidama. Cherries were floated, hand-sorted and dried slowly on raised beds for 18–22 days, with regular turning during the hottest parts of the day.

Recommended brewing: 15.5g coffee to 250g water, 93°C, paper filter. Rest 7–14 days from roast date for best clarity.
