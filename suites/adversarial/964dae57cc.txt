Tasting Notes
strawberry jam, bergamot, cacao nib, honey

This lot is all the reasons we love high elevation Guji: perfumed florals up front, a round berry sweetness through the middle, and a clean cocoa finish as it cools. We roasted this coffee with a light profile for clarity and sweetness on pour-over and batch brew.

Benti Nenka washing station works with smallholder growers around Hambela Wamena who cultivate Ethiopian landrace selections 74110 and 74112 on steep garden plots. Ripe cherries were hand sorted and dried slowly on raised beds for 18-22 days, with careful turning during the heat of the day.
