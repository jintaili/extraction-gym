A clean and luminous washed Ethiopia from the Halo Beriti collection site in Gedeb. In the cup we find lifted jasmine aromatics, Meyer lemon brightness, white peach sweetness, and a long honeyed finish. We roast this profile lightly to keep the florals transparent and the acidity crisp.

Halo Beriti serves smallholder growers farming garden plots around the highland kebeles of Gedeb. Ripe cherries are delivered daily, depulped, fermented under water, washed clean, and dried on raised beds with careful sorting throughout the drying period.

Recommended brew: 16g coffee to 260g water, 93°C, flat-bottom brewer.
