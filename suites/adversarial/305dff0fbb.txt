This tiny-format release is for drinkers who want to taste one of our favorite lots before committing to a full bag. The 50g sampler is enough for one generous pourover or two smaller brews; the coffee inside is the same roast as the 250g and 1kg options.

El Vergel sits on the slopes outside Fresno in Tolima, where the Bayter family has been refining careful picking and long, clean drying protocols. This washed Gesha opens with a perfumed jasmine aroma, then moves into white peach and bergamot acidity with a clear cane-sugar finish.

We roast this lot light to preserve its florals and transparent citrus sweetness. Best brewed as filter coffee after 7-21 days off roast.
