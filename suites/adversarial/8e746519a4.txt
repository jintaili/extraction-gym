Tasting Notes: mango nectar, key lime, jasmine, passionfruit sorbet
Roast: Light filter roast
Producer: Jhoan Vergara, Finca Las Flores
Country: Colombia
Region: Huila, Pitalito
Process: Anaerobic Natural
Variety: Pink Bourbon
Altitude: 1,750–1,850 masl
Harvest: November 2024

This tiny release from Finca Las Flores is all sunshine and lift: a syrupy cup that opens with tropical sweetness, then snaps into lime-like acidity and a clean floral finish. We are roasting it lightly to keep the aromatics high and the body silky rather than heavy.

Jhoan Vergara and his family have become known for very controlled fermentations. For this lot, ripe Pink Bourbon cherries were hand sorted, placed in sealed fermentation tanks, and layered with fresh passionfruit pulp and a small amount of dried orange peel before drying on raised beds. The fruit is not a flavouring added after roasting; it is part of the fermentation environment at origin, which is why the cup reads so vividly of ripe tropical fruit while still tasting like beautiful Colombian coffee.

Finca Las Flores sits above Pitalito in southern Huila, where warm afternoons and cool nights help Pink Bourbon develop dense sweetness. We recommend brewing with a slightly lower dose than usual if you want the citrus to pop.

Brew recipe
V60: 15g coffee / 250g water / 93°C / 2:45 total brew time
