A clean, luminous Pink Bourbon from Finca La Esperanza in southern Huila. Ana Maria Ramirez picks ripe cherry in small passes, ferments the depulped coffee for 28 hours, then washes it in fresh spring water before slow drying on raised beds under shade cloth.

In the cup we find red currant acidity, orange blossom aromatics, and a panela-like sweetness that carries through a silky finish. We are roasting this lot light for clarity and sweetness on pour-over and batch brew.

This is not an espresso blend and it is not decaffeinated. Limited single-farm lot; roasted Mondays and Thursdays in Portland, Oregon.
