Tasting Notes: brown sugar, pear, cocoa nib

Producer: Asociación La Serranía
Region: Huila, Colombia
Process: Sugarcane EA
Variety: Castillo, Caturra
Altitude: 1,650–1,900 masl
Harvest: November 2024–January 2025
Roast: Filter / Omni

A sweet and easy-drinking Colombian lot from smallholder members of Asociación La Serranía in the hills outside Pitalito. We taste soft pear acidity, brown sugar sweetness, and a clean cocoa finish that makes this a reliable everyday brew across pour-over, batch, and immersion recipes.

The lots for this release were selected for balance and clarity, then prepared using the Sugarcane EA method before roasting gently on our Loring in small batches. Expect a round cup with low bitterness and a mellow finish.

Brew suggestion
18g coffee / 300g water / 94°C / medium grind
