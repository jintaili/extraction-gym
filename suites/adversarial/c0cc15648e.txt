Our current Colombian single origin comes from Marta Rojas and her family at Finca Las Brisas, a small hillside farm outside Acevedo in southern Huila. The Pink Bourbon trees are picked at deep red ripeness, floated, depulped, fermented in tile tanks for 36 hours, washed clean, and dried slowly on raised beds under shade cloth.

In the cup we find a bright, structured sweetness: white peach up front, lemon curd acidity through the middle, and a cane-sugar finish with a light jasmine aroma as the coffee cools.
