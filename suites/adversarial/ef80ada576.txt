A vivid washed Pacamara from the Aguirre family's Finca El Injerto in Huehuetenango. This lot opens with fragrant jasmine and a clear blood orange acidity, finishing with a soft panela sweetness. We have roasted it for filter brewing to highlight the florals and the structured citrus character that makes this farm so loved.

About the farm
Finca El Injerto sits high in the mountains of Huehuetenango and has been operated by the Aguirre family for generations. The farm separates varieties and elevations carefully, and this Pacamara selection was fully washed after fermentation before being dried slowly on patios.

Recommended brew recipe
For V60 we like: 18g coffee in, 300g water out, 93°C, 2:45–3:00 total brew time. For espresso, try another coffee from our espresso range — this roast is dialled for filter.

Shipping & returns
Orders roast and ship Monday to Thursday. Wholebean is packed in nitrogen-flushed retail bags with a one-way valve.
