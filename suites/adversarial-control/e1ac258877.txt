This bright and polished lot comes from Kanzu Washing Station in the hills above Lake Kivu. We roasted it for filter brewing to keep the cup transparent: red currant acidity, mandarin sweetness, black tea structure, brown sugar, and a clean citrus finish.

Kanzu works with smallholder growers around Nyamasheke who deliver ripe Red Bourbon cherries throughout the harvest. After hand sorting, the coffee is depulped, fully washed, fermented in tiled tanks, and dried slowly on raised beds. The result is a precise washed Rwanda profile with clarity and a long, sweet finish.

Recommended brew: 1:16 ratio, 94°C water, medium-fine grind. Best enjoyed 7–28 days off roast.
