About this coffee
La Bolsa sits outside La Libertad in Huehuetenango, where cool nights and limestone-rich soils make for a structured, sweet cup. This lot is a straightforward washed separation of Bourbon and Caturra, selected for cafe batch brew and retail refill bins where consistency matters.

We roast this coffee to a medium-light profile: developed enough for caramel and milk chocolate sweetness, but still bright with red apple and a soft orange blossom finish. It is not a blend and it is not decaffeinated.
