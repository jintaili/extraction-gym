This small lot from Grace Morales' Finca Cruz Loma is one of the clearest Ecuadorian coffees we've roasted this season. The washed process keeps the cup bright and layered: first tangerine and apricot, then a delicate jasmine lift, with panela sweetness carrying through the finish.

Grace farms on the cloud-forest slopes outside Nanegalito, where cool nights and slow maturation help preserve acidity. We are roasting this as a light filter profile to keep the florals forward while building enough sweetness for an easy morning cup.

Recommended brew
V60: 16g coffee, 260g water, 94C, 2:45-3:00 total time.
For espresso, we recommend our house blend instead; this coffee is profiled for filter brewing.
