This lot comes from Finca El Mirador, a small hillside farm outside Palestina in Huila. Ana Maria Gomez separates her Pink Bourbon cherries by day lot before a long, clean fermentation and full washing in cool mountain water. The result is a structured, high-sweetness cup with peach-like acidity and a panela finish.

We roast this coffee for filter brewing: enough development for sweetness and clarity, without pushing it toward an espresso profile. If you are shopping specifically for espresso or decaf coffees, those collection links live in the footer navigation; this coffee is neither decaffeinated nor our espresso roast.

Recommended brew: 1:16.5 ratio, 94°C water, medium-fine grind.
