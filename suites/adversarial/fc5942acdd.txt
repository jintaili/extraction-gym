This washed lot from Chelbesa is the kind of coffee that made us fall in love with coffees from Gedeb: transparent, floral, and sweet without feeling delicate. In the cup we find jasmine aromatics up front, a lemon-curd brightness, ripe apricot sweetness, and a long black-tea finish.

Chelbesa washing station receives freshly picked cherries from smallholder growers farming garden plots around the kebele. Cherries are sorted on delivery, depulped, fermented in water, washed clean, and dried slowly on raised beds. The result is a crisp and structured cup that shines as a pour over or batch brew.

Recommended recipe: 20g coffee to 320g water, 202°F, medium-fine grind. Rest 7–14 days from roast date for best clarity.
