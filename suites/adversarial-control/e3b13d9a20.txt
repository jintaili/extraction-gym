This lot comes from Ana Milena Rivera's Finca La Esperanza in Palestina, Huila, where Pink Bourbon is grown on steep, shaded slopes above the town. The cup is delicate and structured: ripe stone fruit up front, a floral jasmine aroma, panela sweetness, and a clean lime-zest finish.

Ana's washed process is intentionally simple and precise. Cherries are hand sorted, floated, depulped the same evening, fermented in sealed tanks for 32 hours, then washed and dried on raised beds for 18 days. We roast this coffee light for clarity and sweetness, best brewed as pour-over, batch brew, or AeroPress.

Brew recommendation
1:16 ratio, 94°C water, medium-fine grind. Let the cup cool a little for the peach and florals to open.
