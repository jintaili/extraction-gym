This washed lot comes from smallholder growers around Bombe in the Bensa district of Sidama. Cherries are delivered daily to Qonqona Washing Station, where they are floated, depulped, fermented in water, and dried slowly on raised beds.

We love this coffee for its clean, tea-like structure and sparkling citrus sweetness. Expect peach tea, Meyer lemon, jasmine honey, and a soft bergamot finish as the cup cools. Roasted for filter brewing, but also lovely as a bright single-origin espresso if you enjoy higher-acid espresso.

Recommended brew: 16:1 ratio, 94°C water, medium-fine grind.
