Peach, blueberry jam, jasmine, cacao nib

Producer: Bombe Village smallholder producers
Washing Station: Damo Station
Origin: Ethiopia
Region: Sidama, Bensa, Bombe
Process: Natural
Variety: JARC 74158, 74110
Altitude: 1,950 - 2,150 masl
Harvest: January - February 2025
Roast: Light

This lot comes from smallholder growers around Bombe village in the Bensa district of Sidama. Cherries are delivered to Damo Station on the day of picking, floated for density, and dried slowly on raised beds for 18-22 days with careful turning through the first week.

In the cup we find a layered natural profile: ripe peach up front, blueberry jam sweetness, a soft jasmine aroma, and a clean cacao nib finish. We roast it light to preserve the florals and keep the fruit bright rather than boozy.

Brew recommendation: start at 16:1 with 94°C water. This coffee shines as a filter brew and also makes a juicy, high-acidity espresso if you enjoy lighter profiles.
