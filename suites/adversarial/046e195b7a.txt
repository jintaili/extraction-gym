Roast: Filter roast

Origin: Colombia
Region: Aponte, Nariño

Process: red honey
Variety: Caturra, Castillo, Colombia
Altitude: 1,900–2,150 masl
Harvest: September–November 2024

This red honey lot comes from the Inga community around Aponte in the highlands of Nariño. Ripe cherries are floated, depulped with a generous amount of mucilage left intact, and dried slowly on raised beds until the moisture is stable.

In the cup we taste a layered red-fruit sweetness that reminds us of strawberry jam, panela, hibiscus tea and a crisp red apple finish. We roast this coffee for filter brewing, aiming for clarity and a soft, syrupy texture rather than heavy development.

About the producers
The Inga are an Indigenous community farming small parcels on steep volcanic slopes near Aponte. Coffee is collected as parchment from participating families and kept together as a traceable community lot by the local association.

Brew recipe
V60: 16g coffee / 260g water / 2:45–3:15 total time.
