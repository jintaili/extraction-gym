Tasting notes: blood orange, panela, jasmine

Roast level: Light

Producer: Maria Fernanda Arias, Finca La Estrella
Region: Palestina, Huila
Country: Colombia
Process: Washed
Variety: Pink Bourbon
Altitude: 1,780-1,920 masl
Harvest: May-July 2025

A bright and deeply sweet Huila coffee from Maria Fernanda Arias, whose family farm sits above Palestina on the eastern slopes of the Magdalena valley. The cup opens with blood orange and jasmine aromatics, then settles into panela sweetness and a clean black-tea finish.

For this lot, ripe Pink Bourbon cherries were hand-sorted, depulped the same afternoon, fermented in tiled tanks for 30 hours, and washed with cool spring water before slow drying on raised beds. We roasted it lightly to preserve the floral structure and citrus clarity.

Recommended for: filter brewing. Not roasted for espresso.
