This washed lot from Finca La Bolsa is a clean, structured Guatemala we roast for everyday wholesale menus. In the cup we find red apple and orange blossom up front, with a caramel sweetness that carries through a crisp finish. It is approachable enough for batch brew but still has the clarity we want on a rotating single-origin bar.

La Bolsa sits high in Huehuetenango, where cool nights and limestone-rich soils help the Bourbon and Caturra trees develop dense, sweet cherries. The coffee is depulped, fermented, washed, and dried slowly on patios before being prepared for export.
