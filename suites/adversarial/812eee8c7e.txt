Variety: Pink Bourbon
Altitude: 1,750–1,900 masl
Harvest: June–August 2024

This Pink Bourbon lot from El Diviso is the kind of clean, high-definition Colombian coffee we wait all season for. In the cup we find a saturated strawberry sweetness, bright Meyer lemon, and a soft raw-sugar finish that keeps the profile elegant rather than heavy.

Nestor Lasso and his family cultivate small hillside parcels near Bruselas in Huila, picking selectively for ripe cherries before a careful washed fermentation and slow drying on raised beds. We are roasting this lot lightly for filter brewing to preserve its floral acidity and layered fruit character.

Recommended for: V60, Kalita, batch brew, AeroPress.
