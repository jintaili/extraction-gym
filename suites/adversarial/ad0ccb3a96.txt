About this coffee
La Cordillera is named for the mountain spine that links the two lots in this release. On the cup table it drinks with the poise of a clean single origin: crisp apple acidity, panela sweetness and a soft florality that makes it an easy everyday filter coffee.

Although the front label keeps the origin simple, La Cordillera is a single-country seasonal blend assembled from two Colombian components: washed Caturra and Colombia from smallholders around San Agustín, Huila, and a naturally processed Castillo lot from La Unión, Nariño. We built the blend to keep the cup lively as fresh arrivals rotate through the season.

Blend composition:
- 65% Huila, San Agustín smallholders — washed — Caturra, Colombia — 1,750–2,050 masl
- 35% Nariño, La Unión smallholders — natural — Castillo — 1,650–1,900 masl

18g coffee / 300g water / 94°C / 2:45 total brew time
