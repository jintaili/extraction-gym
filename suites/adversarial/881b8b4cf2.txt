This lot comes to us from the high-elevation community of Hamasho in Bensa, Sidama. We are roasting it light to keep the florals up front and the cup clean, with a sweet lemon-candy acidity and an apricot-like finish as it cools.

At the Hamasho washing station, smallholder growers deliver ripe cherries grown around 2,200 masl and above. The coffee is depulped, fermented in water, washed, and dried slowly on raised beds. The result is a delicate washed Ethiopia with jasmine aromatics, black-tea structure, and a sparkling citrus sweetness.

Please note: the Sampler 50g size is intended for cupping or trying a coffee before committing to a full bag. For regular brewing, select 250g or 1kg.
