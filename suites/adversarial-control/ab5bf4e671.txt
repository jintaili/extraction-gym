This lot from Luis Anibal Calderón is a clear, lifted expression of Pink Bourbon grown above Palestina in southern Huila. After hand sorting, the cherries were depulped, fermented in tiled tanks, washed with mountain spring water, and dried slowly on raised beds.

In the cup we find a floral, tea-like structure with peach sweetness and a clean panela finish. We roast it for filter brewing, aiming for clarity and a soft, lingering sweetness rather than heavy development.

About the producer
Luis Anibal Calderón and his family farm Finca El Mirador in Huila, where they have focused on small separations of distinctive varieties such as Pink Bourbon. This selection was picked at peak ripeness and milled on the farm before export.
