A tiny bag for a proper look. Our 100g Sampler is the default size on this page so you can try this lot before committing to a full bag; choose 250g or 1kg above if you already know it is for you.

This Pink Bourbon lot comes from Nestor Lasso and the team at Finca El Diviso in Bruselas, Huila. Cherries were hand-picked at peak ripeness, floated and carefully fermented before washing and slow drying on raised beds. The cup is bright and layered: raspberry jam up front, a clean orange-blossom florality, and a panela sweetness that carries through the finish.

We roast this coffee for filter brewing, aiming for clarity rather than weight. Recommended for V60, Kalita, AeroPress and batch brew.
