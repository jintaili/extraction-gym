Coffee details
Origin: Kenya
Region: Nyeri
Producer: Gichathaini Factory, Gikanda Farmers Cooperative Society
Process: Fully washed
Variety: SL-28, SL-34, Ruiru 11
Altitude: 1,700–1,900 masl
Harvest: October–December 2024
Roast: Filter roast

Tasting notes: Blackcurrant, pink grapefruit, cane sugar

A bright, articulate Kenyan lot for wholesale bars that want a dependable filter offering with plenty of sparkle. In the cup we find blackcurrant cordial, pink grapefruit acidity and a clean cane-sugar finish. The roast profile is developed for batch brew, hand brew and retail shelves, not espresso.

Gichathaini Factory is one of the washing stations operated by Gikanda Farmers Cooperative Society in Nyeri. Smallholder members deliver ripe cherry grown on the red volcanic soils around the slopes of Mount Kenya. After pulping, the coffee is fermented, washed in clean water channels and dried slowly on raised beds.
