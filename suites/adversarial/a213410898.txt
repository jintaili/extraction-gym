About this coffee
This lot comes from Finca Los Cedros, a small farm above the town of Cajamarca in the department of Tolima, Colombia. Ana Lucía Rojas grows Caturra and Castillo on steep volcanic soils, picking ripe cherry in multiple passes during the main harvest.

After sorting, the coffee is depulped the same day, fermented in tiled tanks for 28 hours, washed with clean spring water, and dried slowly on raised beds under shade cloth. We roast this as a light filter profile to highlight yellow peach up front, panela sweetness in the middle, and a floral jasmine finish with a lime-like sparkle.
