A bright and fragrant washed Ethiopia from the Danche Washing Station in Chelbesa. We taste jasmine aromatics, white peach sweetness, a clean Meyer lemon sparkle, and a honeyed finish that makes this lot shine on pour-over.

Danche Washing Station serves smallholder farmers around Chelbesa in Gedeb, Gedeo. Freshly delivered cherries are depulped, fermented in clean water, washed through grading channels, and dried slowly on raised beds until the parchment reaches export-ready moisture.

This is roasted light for clarity and sweetness. We recommend a 1:16 ratio, soft water, and a flat-bottom dripper to highlight the floral cup.
