A bright washed lot from the hills above Bombe village in Bensa, roasted for filter brewing.

Tasting Notes: Apricot, jasmine, lemon candy, black tea
Roast Level: Light
Origin: Ethiopia
Region: Bensa, Sidama
Producer: Bombe Village smallholders
Process: Washed
Variety: 74158
Altitude: 1,920–2,020 masl
Harvest: 2024 harvest
Recommended for: Filter

This coffee comes from smallholder growers around Bombe village, where ripe cherry is delivered to a local washing station and sorted before fermentation, washing, and slow drying on raised beds. The high elevation and cool nights give the cup a lifted floral aroma and a clean tea-like finish.

We roast this lot lightly to emphasize clarity and citrus sweetness. Expect a delicate cup with apricot, jasmine, lemon candy, and black tea when brewed as pour-over or batch brew.

Ships whole bean unless you select a grind option at checkout.
