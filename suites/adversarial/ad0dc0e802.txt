Origin: Colombia
Region: Huila, Palestina

Variety: Pink Bourbon
Altitude: 1,750-1,900 masl
Harvest: July-September 2024

A tiny but memorable lot from Finca El Diviso, this Pink Bourbon is the coffee we keep brewing for visitors when we want to show how delicate Colombian coffees can be. In the cup we find white peach and lime zest up front, a lifted jasmine aroma, and a honeycomb sweetness that carries through the finish.

Nestor Lasso and his family have built Finca El Diviso into one of Huila's most talked-about farms by separating small day-lots and drying each one slowly under shade. This washed separation was picked at peak ripeness, fermented in sealed tanks, rinsed with clean mountain water, and dried on raised beds.

We roast this coffee light for filter brewing. The 100g sampler is selected by default so you can taste the lot before committing to a full bag; choose 250g or 2 lb if you already know you want more.

Recommended brew: V60 / Kalita / batch brewer
