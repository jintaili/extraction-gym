This washed lot comes to us from the Chelbesa Washing Station in the Gedeo Zone, where smallholder farmers deliver ripe cherries from garden plots surrounding the village. The station sorts cherries by density, depulps the same day, and ferments the parchment before washing and drying it slowly on raised beds.

In the cup we find an intensely floral profile with jasmine aromatics, a white peach sweetness, Meyer lemon acidity, and a clean black tea finish. We roast this coffee light for wholesale filter service, keeping the texture transparent and the citrus notes bright.
