This clean, high-elevation Rwanda is from smallholder growers delivering ripe Red Bourbon cherry to Gitwe Hill washing station in Gakenke. The lot is built from day lots separated for density and sweetness, then fully washed and dried slowly on raised beds.

In the cup we taste tart cranberry up front, a floral orange-blossom lift, and panela sweetness that settles into a black-tea finish. We roast this lot lightly to keep the citrusy structure and delicate florals intact.

Suggested brew: 16g coffee / 260g water, 94°C, V60 or Kalita. Ships whole bean by default. Leave a note at checkout if you need it ground.
