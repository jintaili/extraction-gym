This washed lot from Worka Chelbesa is everything we hope for from fresh-crop Gedeo coffees: crystalline citrus, a tea-like body, and florals that keep opening as the cup cools. We are tasting bergamot, lemon candy, peach tea, and jasmine, with a clean honeyed finish.

The Worka Chelbesa washing station serves smallholder growers around Gedeb, where garden plots are planted with 74110 and 74112 selections. Cherries are hand-sorted before depulping, fermented under clean water, and dried slowly on raised beds. The high elevation and careful washing give this coffee its lifted aromatics and precise acidity.

Recommended for: pour over, batch brew, AeroPress
Not recommended as a dedicated espresso roast.
