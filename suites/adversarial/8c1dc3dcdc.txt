A clean washed Ethiopia from the Worka Chelbesa area, built around sparkling citrus and soft florals. In the cup we find jasmine aromatics, white peach sweetness, and a lemon-curd finish that cools toward bergamot tea.

Worka Chelbesa Washing Station receives ripe cherry from smallholder farmers around Gedeb. Coffee is depulped, fermented in water, washed, and dried slowly on raised beds to preserve clarity and sweetness.

Recommended for: filter brewing, pour over, batch brew
Not roasted for espresso.
