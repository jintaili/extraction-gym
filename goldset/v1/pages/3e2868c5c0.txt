Title: "Crowd Pleaser" House Espresso | Coffee Project New York
Description: We Taste:Black cherry, chocolate bar, honey. Medium acidity, syrupy body.The Crowd Pleaser Espresso is a blend of two component coffees: a natural Guatemala from a cooperative in Ayarza and a washed Colombia from a smallholder farm in Huila. We have cultivated ongoing relationships with both of these producers since 2020 and have grown together to earn the title “relationship coffee.” Over the years, the farm in Colombia has grown from 4 families to 12; long-term support like ours allows for greater sales stability and therefore opportunities to invest in resources, education, quality, and new initiatives. Our continued business contributes to uplifting entire communities with coffee.While both of these coffees can hold their own as quality single origins, we incorporate them into blends to elevate our house signatures, which are just as important to us as the finest single origin Panama Gesha. In the Crowd Pleaser, these coffees unite in a blend built for versatility and blending with milk, ice, sugar, and more.Sizes: 10 oz. / 2 lb. / 5 lb.Please note that our 2 lb. and 5 lb. coffee are packed in side-gusseted bags with valve. 2 lb. and 5 lb. bags are not available for purchase in store.
