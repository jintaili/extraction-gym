This AA lot comes from the Gichathaini Factory in the Mathira division of Nyeri, where smallholder members of Gikanda FCS deliver ripe cherry from the surrounding hillsides. After pulping, the coffee is fermented, washed in channels, and dried slowly on raised beds under shade during the hottest part of the day.

In the cup we find the classic Nyeri profile we wait for every year: vivid blackcurrant sweetness, sparkling pink grapefruit acidity, and a clean raw-sugar finish. It is bright, structured, and best enjoyed as a filter coffee.

Recommended brew recipe
Kalita Wave 185: Dose 18g coffee, 300g water at 94°C, total brew time 3:00–3:20. Grind medium-fine and pour in four even pulses.
